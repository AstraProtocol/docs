# Connecting MetaMask to Astra Protocol

In the following step-by-step guide, you will learn how to use the MetaMask chrome extension to send/receive and interact with the Astra chain.

# Installing and setting up MetaMask

MetaMask can be downloaded on Chrome and Firefox, or on iOS and Android if you’re a mobile user.

Firstly, you’ll want to head over to the MetaMask Download page (opens new window). From there, select whichever platform you’re using, and follow the steps to install it on your device. Easy!

Next, follow along with the setup specified by the app. Go ahead and click Create a Wallet. Write down the backup seed phrase (opens new window) somewhere secret (preferably not on an Internet-connected device). Without this phrase, your funds can’t be recovered if your device is damaged or lost. Confirm that you’ve written them down on the next page.

And that’s it! You should now see your wallet, ready to send and receive funds.

The freshly-initialized MetaMask wallet.

# Configuring the wallet

Let’s set up!

  • Hit the my account button in the top right corner, under "Settings", select "Networks"
